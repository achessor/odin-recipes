Simple recipe for Chicken & Rice!

An easy recipe for chicken and rice using an instapot. It only takes approx 25 minutes from start to finish. You will need an InstaPot for this dish.

This is one of my favorite dishes. It is quick from start to finish and you don't need many ingredients. It also stores well in the refrigerator and you can eat it throughtout the week. Eat within 5 days.

Ingredients

  • InstaPot
  • 2 Boneless chicken breasts
  • 1 Ranch seasoning packet
  • 1 carton of Chicken Broth
  • 12oz of jasmine long-grain rice

Instructions for making InstaPot Chicken&Rice

  1. Preheat the InstaPot to High
  2. Set timer for 20 minutes
  3. Place both chicken breasts into InstaPot with the chicken broth
  4. Once timer is up, add the long-grain rice
  5. Cook for 6 mins and then add the ranch seasoning packet
