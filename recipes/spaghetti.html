Spaghetti Recipe!

An easy recipe for cooking spaghetti at home! Only takes about 30 minutes

Cooking spaghetti at home is quite easy. You only need a few ingredients. It stores well for up to 5 days and I think that its even better after a couple of days.

Ingredients

  • Your favorite noodles
  • Spaghetti Sauce
  • 1lb of ground beef
  • Salt & Pepper

Instructions for making Spaghetti!

  1. Get out a frying pan & a large cooking pot
  2. Start boiling water, enough to cover the noodles and have a little extra room
  3. Drop noodles in boiling water
  4. Put your ground beef in the frying pan on Med-High heat.
  5. Once your noodles are soft, strain the water from the pot
  6. Take your meat out of the frying pan and drain extra liquid
